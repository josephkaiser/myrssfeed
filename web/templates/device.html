Connect this device

myRSSfeed uses a private certificate. Your browser will show a security warning the first time — that's expected. Just proceed through it once and you're done.

  1. Open Safari and go to https://myrssfeed.local.
  2. Tap Show Details on the warning page, then visit this website.
  3. Confirm when prompted — Safari will remember your choice.

Use Safari — Chrome and Firefox on iOS cannot bypass this prompt the same way.

  1. Open Chrome and go to https://myrssfeed.local.
  2. Tap Advanced on the warning page, then Proceed to myrssfeed.local.
  1. Open Safari and go to https://myrssfeed.local.
  2. Click Show Details, then visit this website.
  3. Enter your password when prompted — Safari will remember this.
  1. Open Edge and go to https://myrssfeed.local.
  2. Click Advanced on the warning page, then Continue to myrssfeed.local.
